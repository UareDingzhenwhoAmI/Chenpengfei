Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the platform cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting at $19.99/month for individual users and $29.99/month for family plans. Enterprise solutions for healthcare providers and employers are available upon request.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to sign up for the trial.

Can I use the platform with my existing health devices?

Absolutely! Our platform integrates with popular health devices including Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, and many more. We also support data import from Google Fit and Apple Health.

Is my family's health data secure?

Yes. We take data security very seriously. All health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We comply with HIPAA regulations and follow strict data privacy practices.

Do you offer family discounts?

Yes! Our family plan includes features for up to 6 family members at a discounted rate compared to individual plans. We also offer additional discounts for annual subscriptions.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing cycle.
